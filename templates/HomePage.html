Who are we?

Ako Connect is a student led initiative that was established in 2025, which included students who were high achievers academically,
attaining an Excellence endorsement in Level 1 NCEA.

This group of students from the cohort of 2027, recognised a problem within the school - students did not want to study.
However, for the students who did want to study, we found that many struggled due to the inability to easily find resources,
such as past papers, or other material that they needed to learn.

We here at Ako Connect believe that if we are able to fix this problem,
create a hub where students can find resources for their studies easily, offering personal help to students such as tutoring,
we will able to increase the amount of students who want to study, and increase their overall grades.

On this site, you will be able to find helpful websites and youtube videos that many students have reported to be very beneficial with their studies.
Another key feature of this site is the ability to book 1-on-1 tutoring sessions with senior students.
These students are high achieving academically, and have given up their time to offer tutoring to other students.
We also believe that having student tutors as opposed to adults will incentivise more students to ask for help, as they may feel more comfortable with fellow students.


Feel free to navigate this website and utilise the resources we have for you.

This website is currently in early development.
