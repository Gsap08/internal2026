Resources

In this page you will be able to find links to past papers, youtube videos, and other resources we have collated for you to ues in your studies.

Feel free to look through the resources and use them for your studies.

Good luck and have fun studying!

Helpful Websites:

Ourexams.org

ourexams.org is a student made website that was made by a group of students from Nayland college.

Here you can find many past papers collated in one area.

To use, search up the subject or assessment number you are looking for, and you can download the past 10 years past papers in one click.

Use this for studying and revising past exam questions, with the convenience of it all being in one spot.

Click this link to visit ourexams.org

No Brain Too Small

No Brain Too Small, also known as NB2S or NBTS, is a website that is made purely for learning science

This website contains an abundance of information regarding NCEA Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and General Science.

This is a very helpful place to learn, for notes, and to find past papers to revise.

Click this link to visit No Brain Too Small
